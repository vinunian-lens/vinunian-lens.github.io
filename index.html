A Study in Student Identity at VinUniversity

Mapping Student Life at VinUniversity:
The Place That Shapes Our Paths

What is the map that best leads to a successful undergraduate journey at VinUniversity?

Passion is not always something students bring with them when they arrive at university. Sometimes, it is something they gradually discover through places they pass, people they meet, and experiences they do not expect.

To explore this question, we will now analyze the journey of a Cohort 2 CECS student graduating this year (2026). His story is not meant to represent a single “correct” path, but rather one example of how uncertainty, friendships, pauses, and new opportunities can slowly shape a student’s identity at VinUniversity.
